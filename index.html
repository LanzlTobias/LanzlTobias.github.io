Welcome to my website!

Welcome to my website, where I showcase my data science projects. While the page is currently under construction, I appreciate your patience as I work on adding more content.

As an aspiring data scientist, I embarked on my professional journey in the field of research, driven by a deep desire to apply and expand my knowledge and skills. My career initially began as a lab technician, but it was during my studies in agricultural and horticultural sciences at the Technical University of Munich that I discovered my true passion for statistics and programming.

It was there that I wrote my thesis, delving into the bioinformatic analysis of gene flow between wild tomato species in the esteemed lab of Aurelien Tellier back in 2019.

Following this, I secured a position as a research assistant in the plant breeding department, under the guidance of Chris-Carolin Schoen. Throughout my tenure, I focused on simulating breeding programs and predicting the genetic value of maize lines. Alongside this research, I had the opportunity to share my knowledge by teaching classes on quantitative genetics, statistical concepts, and programming using R.

Since March 2023, I work a Junior Data Visualization Specialist at Hopmann Marketing.

As a dedicated professional in Data Visualization and Data Analytics, I possess a solid foundation in R, Microsoft Power BI, and data visualization techniques. I possess a keen eye for transforming intricate data into meaningful visualizations that facilitate informed decision-making.

I thrive on leveraging data to uncover actionable insights and have successfully contributed to projects across diverse domains, including marketing analytics and plant breeding.

Stay tuned for more updates and projects on this website. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing my work with you soon.
